How to Convert PDF to TEXT?

1. Click the Choose Files button to upload PDF files.

Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my file?

▼

Your files are completely secure. We use advanced encryption to protect your data during upload and conversion. Files are automatically deleted from our servers after processing.

What file formats do you support?

▼

▼

No, we do not store your files after conversion. All files are automatically deleted from our servers within 24 hours to ensure your privacy.
