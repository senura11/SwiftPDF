Frequently Asked Questions

How secure is my file?

Your files are completely secure. We use advanced encryption to protect your data during upload and conversion. Files are automatically deleted from our servers after processing.

Is this service free to use?

Yes, our basic conversion service is completely free. We also offer premium features for advanced users with additional requirements.

What's the maximum file size limit?

For free users, the maximum file size is 100MB. Premium users can convert files up to 500MB.

How long does conversion take?

Most conversions are completed within seconds. Larger files may take a few minutes depending on the file size and conversion type.

Which formats are supported?

We support a wide range of formats including DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, JPG, PNG, SVG, TXT, and more. Check our format selector for the complete list.

Can I convert multiple files at once?

Yes, premium users can convert multiple files simultaneously. Free users can convert one file at a time.
